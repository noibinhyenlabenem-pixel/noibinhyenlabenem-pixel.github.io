PRIVACY POLICY FOR FLASH BOMB

Effective Date: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you play the "Flash Bomb" mobile game.

2. Information We Collect
We DO NOT collect any personal identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
We only collect non-personal data necessary for displaying advertisements:
- Device Identifiers: We collect Device IDs (such as Apple's IDFA) to serve relevant ads.
- Advertising Data: We collect information about ad views and app interactions via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services
Our game uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data to personalize ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Tracking & Opt-Out
We respect your privacy. You can opt-out of personalized advertising at any time by selecting "Ask App Not to Track" when prompted on your iOS device, or by adjusting the tracking settings in your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official App Store support link.
